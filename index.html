Installing Spotify Friend Activity: A Step-by-Step Guide

Now, let's install the Spotify Friend Activity extension on your device. Further, follow these steps to start monitoring your friends' listening habits effortlessly:

  • Add the Extension to Chrome Firstly, start the installation by clicking the "Add to Chrome" button.
  • Confirm the Installation Next, click the “Add Extension” button on your screen.
  • Access the Extensions Menu Next, click the “Puzzle” icon on the right side of the Chrome toolbar.
  • Pin the Extension Then, pin the Spotify Friend Activity extension by clicking the blue pin icon next to its name.
  • Log In to Spotify Continue by heading to open.spotify.com and logging into your account.
  • Activate the Extension Finally, click on the Spotify Friend Activity Chrome extension icon. You can toggle the Friend Activity features on or off as desired.
